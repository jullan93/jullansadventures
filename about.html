WHO AM I?

I’m a woman in my early 30's who has always had a deep connection with nature and the outdoors. Some of my earliest and happiest memories are of being outside, soaking in the fresh air and freedom that only the natural world can offer. While life took me in different directions for a while, the last six years have brought me back to where my heart belongs — exploring the outdoors, especially hiking in the mountains and camping under the stars.

My grandfather has called me “Jullan” for as long as I can remember, and that nickname feels perfectly tied to my identity as an adventurer and lover of the outdoors. These days, I spend as much time as I can exploring the incredible Nordic landscapes. From the rugged mountain trails to the quiet beauty of a forest at dusk, there’s nothing that compares to the serenity and inspiration I find in nature.

Sleeping in the mountains has become one of my greatest joys. It’s where I sleep the best, surrounded by the stillness of the wilderness and the comforting sounds of nature. Whether it’s a short day hike to a stunning viewpoint or a multi-day exploration deep into remote terrain, I thrive on the opportunity to disconnect from the everyday and connect with the beauty of the world around me.

Over time, this passion has grown into something I love sharing with others. Through my journeys, I capture the magic of Nordic nature with photography and videography. From dramatic landscapes to tiny, vibrant details in the flora and fauna, I want to show the world why these spaces matter—and why they deserve to be protected. National parks and untouched wilderness areas are treasures we must preserve for future generations.

Along with sharing beautiful imagery, I also enjoy offering practical tips and advice for those looking to embark on their own outdoor adventures. Whether it’s tips on how to pack for an overnight hike, advice on staying safe in unpredictable weather, or sharing detailed hiking routes, I love helping others feel confident about stepping into the wild.

To me, the outdoors isn’t just a destination—it’s a way of life. It’s about the calm that comes with a quiet sunrise on a mountaintop, the sense of accomplishment after a challenging hike, and the deep connection to something greater than ourselves. I hope my stories, photos, and videos inspire you to fall in love with nature too, whether you’re exploring your local trails or dreaming of Nordic landscapes.

Join me on this journey, as I share not only the beauty of the outdoors but also the importance of caring for it.

Let’s explore, learn, and preserve our natural world together!
