Who am I behind the camera?

I’m a woman in my early 30's who has always had a deep connection with nature and the outdoors. Some of my earliest and happiest memories are of being outside, breathing in the fresh air and freedom that only the natural world can offer. While life took me in different directions for a while, the last six years have brought me back to where my heart belongs — exploring the outdoors, especially hiking in the mountains and camping under the stars.

My grandfather has called me “Jullan” for as long as I can remember, and that nickname feels perfectly tied to my identity as an adventurer and lover of the outdoors. These days, I spend as much time as I can exploring the incredible Nordic landscapes. From the rugged mountain trails to the quiet beauty of a forest at dusk, there’s nothing that compares to the serenity and inspiration I find in nature.

Sleeping in the mountains has become one of my greatest joys. It’s where I sleep the best, surrounded by the stillness of the wilderness and the comforting sounds of nature. Whether it’s a short day hike to a stunning viewpoint or a multi-day exploration deep into remote terrain, I thrive on the opportunity to disconnect from the everyday and connect with the beauty of the world around me.

Let’s explore, learn, and preserve our natural world together!

PHOTOGRAPHY

Welcome to a world where every photograph tells a story, where nature's untouched beauty takes center stage. I specialize in high-resolution photography, capturing breathtaking landscapes, vibrant wildlife, serene camping spots, adventurous activities, and inviting interiors from collaboration stays.

My goal is simple: to preserve the authenticity of nature in every shot, showcasing its raw, unfiltered charm. Through my work, I invite you to not just see the beauty but to feel it, inspiring you to explore, connect, and immerse yourself in the great outdoors.

I encourage my followers to interact with my content and support the incredible collaborators who make these experiences possible. Let’s bring the magic of nature and the stories behind each stay to life, together.

Videography

Step into the world of high-resolution videography that brings the wild to life. I specialize in capturing the essence of landscapes, wildlife, camping spots, outdoor adventures, and cozy interior stays—all enhanced by breathtaking drone footage that adds a unique perspective to the story.

Every frame I create reflects the raw beauty of nature, preserving its authenticity and inviting viewers to feel a deeper connection to the world around them. Whether it’s the sweeping views of a mountaintop, the quiet moments in a forest, or the energy of an adventurous getaway, my videos tell stories that inspire exploration.

I also encourage my followers to engage with my work and support the incredible collaborators who make these experiences possible. Together, let’s celebrate the wonders of nature and the places that make it unforgettable.
